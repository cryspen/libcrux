### F4. **Forward NTT layer 7 bridge — lift mismatch**

Layer 7 is structurally novel (sprint-learnings flagged it
"NOT a one-session task").  Concrete blocker:

  - `ntt_at_layer_7` uses `multiply_by_constant_bounded(_, _, -1600)`
    on **plain CBD-sampled** inputs (`|c| ≤ 3`), with
    `-1600 ≡ 1729 = ζ_spec (mod q)` — i.e. the impl uses the
    PLAIN ζ value via `i16_to_spec_fe` (`(x mod q)`).
  - Existing forward layer 1/2/3 bridges use `mont_i16_to_spec_fe`
    (`(x · 169) mod q`).  Under that lift,
    `mont_i16_to_spec_fe(-1600) = 2578 ≠ 1729`, so the natural layer-7
    bridge wants `i16_to_spec_array` (plain), not
    `mont_i16_to_spec_array` (Mont).
  - Reconciling the two for `ntt_binomially_sampled_ring_element`'s
    composed post requires either a per-layer lift-transition lemma or a
    re-scaling argument across the chain — neither exists.  Defer
    until the design choice is made.
